# Password Policy — Requirements and Rotation Schedule

Policy Number: IT-SEC-001
Effective Date: 2026-01-01
Review Date: 2027-01-01
Owner: Information Security

## Scope

This policy applies to all employees, contractors, and third-party vendors with access to ACME Financial systems.

## Password Requirements

All Active Directory passwords must meet the following requirements:

- **Minimum length:** 14 characters
- **Complexity:** Must contain at least one character from three of the following four categories:
  - Uppercase letters (A–Z)
  - Lowercase letters (a–z)
  - Numbers (0–9)
  - Special characters (!, @, #, $, %, etc.)
- **No dictionary words** or common phrases
- **No personal information:** Do not include your name, username, or date of birth
- **No reuse:** You may not reuse any of your last 24 passwords

## Password Rotation

- Passwords expire every **90 days**. You will receive email reminders starting 14 days before expiration.
- Passwords for privileged accounts (admin accounts, service accounts) expire every **60 days**.
- Passwords must be changed immediately if you suspect they have been compromised. Contact the IT Security team at security@acme-financial.com.

## Password Storage

- Do not write passwords on paper or store them in unencrypted files.
- The approved corporate password manager is **1Password for Business** (available in Software Center). Use it for all application passwords.
- Sharing passwords with colleagues is prohibited. Use shared mailboxes, service accounts, or delegated access instead.

## Multi-Factor Authentication

MFA is required for all accounts in addition to a strong password. Refer to the MFA Setup guide for configuration instructions. MFA does not reduce password strength requirements.

## Service Account Passwords

Service account passwords are managed by the IT Operations team. Application owners may not know the plaintext password of their service accounts. Requests for service account credential rotation must be submitted through the IT Service Portal.

## Violations

Failure to comply with this policy may result in account suspension and disciplinary action up to and including termination, in accordance with the Employee Code of Conduct.
