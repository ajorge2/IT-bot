# Bring Your Own Device (BYOD) Policy

## Scope

This policy applies to any personally owned device (smartphone, tablet, or laptop) used to access ACME Financial systems, data, or applications.

## Permitted Uses

Employees may use personal devices to access:
- Corporate email and calendar via the Microsoft Outlook mobile app
- Microsoft Teams
- SharePoint and OneDrive for Business via browser or the OneDrive app
- The IT self-service portal

Personal devices may **not** be used to access:
- Trading systems or market data terminals
- Finance and accounting systems (Workday, SAP)
- HR systems containing employee PII
- Tier 3 or Tier 4 data (see Data Classification Policy) unless explicitly approved in writing by your department head and Compliance

## Mandatory Requirements for BYOD

To use a personal device for corporate access, the following are required:

1. **Device PIN or biometric lock** — a minimum 6-digit PIN, fingerprint, or face ID, with automatic lock after no more than 5 minutes of inactivity.
2. **Intune enrollment** — the device must be enrolled in Microsoft Intune (Company Portal app) for compliance monitoring. Without enrollment, corporate apps will not activate.
3. **Current OS version** — devices running operating systems more than 2 major versions behind the current release are not permitted. IT will block access from non-compliant OS versions.
4. **No jailbreaking or rooting** — jailbroken or rooted devices are automatically blocked by Intune.
5. **Encryption** — device storage must be encrypted (enabled by default on current iOS and Android devices).

## Privacy

ACME Financial has access only to the **corporate data and apps managed through Intune**. The firm cannot access personal photos, messages, call logs, browsing history, or any data outside managed apps. If a device is unenrolled or reported lost, only corporate app data is wiped — personal data is unaffected.

## Lost or Stolen Devices

Report a lost or stolen device immediately to the IT Helpdesk (ext. 4357). IT will perform a selective wipe of corporate apps and data. Delay in reporting may result in disciplinary action if corporate data is subsequently accessed without authorisation.

## Reimbursement

ACME Financial does not reimburse mobile data charges for personal devices. Employees who require a corporate-issued device for their role should speak with their manager, who can submit a request through the IT Portal.

## Withdrawal of Access

The firm reserves the right to withdraw BYOD access at any time, including upon employment termination. Corporate app data will be wiped from the device within 24 hours of termination.

## Acceptance

By enrolling a personal device in Intune, you confirm you have read and agreed to this policy. Questions: contact the IT Helpdesk or your HR Business Partner.
