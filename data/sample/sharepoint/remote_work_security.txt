# Remote Work Security Policy

Policy Number: IT-SEC-007
Effective Date: 2026-03-01
Review Date: 2027-03-01
Owner: Information Security

## Scope

This policy applies to all employees, contractors, and vendors who access ACME Financial systems from outside the corporate office network.

## VPN Requirement

Connection to the corporate VPN (GlobalProtect) is mandatory when accessing any internal ACME Financial system from a remote location. This includes:
- Internal web applications and intranets
- Corporate SharePoint and file shares
- Email via Outlook desktop (Outlook Web Access / OWA is permitted without VPN for email only)
- Internal databases and trading systems

The only systems accessible without VPN are: Microsoft 365 (Teams, OWA, OneDrive for Business), Zoom, and vendor-hosted SaaS tools approved by IT. All other systems require VPN.

## Home Network Security

While ACME Financial cannot mandate your home router configuration, the following are strongly recommended:
- Use WPA3 or WPA2 encryption on your home Wi-Fi (never WEP or open networks)
- Change your router's default admin password
- Keep router firmware updated

## Public Wi-Fi

Accessing corporate systems over public Wi-Fi (airports, cafes, hotels) is permitted only when connected to the corporate VPN. Never access corporate systems without VPN on a public network. If the VPN connection drops, stop work until it reconnects.

## Physical Security at Home

- Position your screen so it is not visible from windows or public spaces if you are handling sensitive data.
- Use a privacy screen filter if you work in shared spaces such as co-working areas.
- Lock your workstation (Win+L / Ctrl+Cmd+Q) when stepping away, even at home.

## Printing Sensitive Documents

Printing confidential or restricted documents to personal home printers is prohibited unless IT has approved a secure home printing arrangement. Contact the helpdesk if you have a legitimate need to print restricted documents remotely.

## Incident Reporting

If you suspect your remote working environment has been compromised (e.g., unknown device on your home network, suspicious activity on company accounts), report it immediately to:
- IT Helpdesk: ext. 4357 or security@acme-financial.com
- Do not wait until you are back in the office.

## Non-Compliance

Employees who access corporate systems in violation of this policy may have remote access revoked pending an investigation.
