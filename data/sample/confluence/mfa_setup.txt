# Setting Up Multi-Factor Authentication (MFA)

Multi-factor authentication (MFA) is mandatory for all ACME Financial employees. MFA is required to log into Microsoft 365, the VPN, and all internal web applications.

## Recommended Method: Microsoft Authenticator App

The Microsoft Authenticator app is the fastest and most reliable MFA method. It supports one-tap push notifications and works without a mobile signal (offline TOTP codes).

### Setup on iOS or Android
1. Download "Microsoft Authenticator" from the App Store or Google Play.
2. On your work computer, go to https://aka.ms/mfasetup (requires you to be signed in to Microsoft 365).
3. Click "Add sign-in method" → select "Authenticator app" → "Download now" → "Next".
4. Open the Authenticator app on your phone → tap the "+" → "Work or school account" → "Scan QR code".
5. Scan the QR code shown on your computer screen.
6. Complete the test notification on your phone to confirm setup.

### Using Authenticator for Sign-In
- **Push notification (recommended):** Tap "Approve" on your phone when prompted. Available when you have an internet connection.
- **One-time code (offline fallback):** Open Authenticator, tap your ACME Financial account. A 6-digit code refreshes every 30 seconds. Enter it when prompted.

## MFA Codes Not Working

If your 6-digit TOTP codes are being rejected:

1. **Check time sync on your phone.** TOTP codes are time-based. If your phone clock is wrong by more than 30 seconds, codes will fail.
   - iOS: Settings → General → Date & Time → enable "Set Automatically"
   - Android: Settings → General Management → Date and Time → enable "Automatic date and time"
2. After enabling automatic time, wait 30 seconds for a new code to generate and try again.
3. If codes still fail after a time sync, open Authenticator → tap your account → "Verify devices" (if available) → "Sync now".

## Backup MFA Methods

You should register at least two MFA methods in case you lose access to your primary device. To add a backup method:

1. Go to https://aka.ms/mfasetup
2. Click "Add sign-in method"
3. Options: phone call to your registered mobile, SMS to registered mobile, backup email (personal email allowed for MFA only)

Important: Backup methods are for MFA only. Your corporate email password must never be sent to a personal account.

## Lost or Replaced Phone

If you get a new phone or lose your current one:

1. Contact the IT Helpdesk immediately (ext. 4357) to temporary bypass MFA and de-register your old device.
2. Set up the Authenticator app on your new phone using the setup steps above.
3. Verify the new method works before the helpdesk removes the bypass.

Do not delay reporting a lost phone — your MFA credentials could be compromised.

## Hardware Token (YubiKey) — Executives and Privileged Accounts

Executives and accounts with privileged access (admin rights, access to trading systems) are required to use a YubiKey hardware token in addition to the Authenticator app. Contact the helpdesk to request a YubiKey if you have not been issued one.
