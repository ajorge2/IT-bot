# How to Reset Your Active Directory Password

Your Active Directory (AD) password controls access to your Windows workstation, email, SharePoint, and most internal applications.

## Self-Service Password Reset (Recommended)

You can reset your own password without calling the helpdesk using the Self-Service Password Reset portal.

1. Go to https://sspr.acme-financial.internal from any browser (including your personal device).
2. Enter your corporate email address (firstname.lastname@acme-financial.com).
3. Choose a verification method: Microsoft Authenticator app, backup email, or SMS to your registered mobile number.
4. Complete the verification challenge.
5. Enter a new password that meets the requirements (see Password Policy).
6. Click Reset Password. The change takes effect within 60 seconds.

Note: If you are locked out of your workstation, you can use a personal device or the kiosk in the IT reception area to access the portal.

## Password Reset at the Helpdesk

If you cannot use the self-service portal (e.g., you have not registered for SSPR), contact the IT Helpdesk:

- Phone: ext. 4357 (HELP) or +1-555-234-4357
- Teams: Search "IT Helpdesk" and send a message
- Walk-in: IT Support Desk, Floor 2, Building A (Mon–Fri, 8am–6pm)

You will be asked to verify your identity with your employee ID and answers to your registered security questions.

## After Resetting Your Password

- Update your password on all enrolled devices (phone, tablet, home workstation if domain-joined).
- If you use the Cisco AnyConnect or GlobalProtect VPN client, you must re-authenticate after a password change. See the VPN Setup guide.
- If Outlook prompts you for credentials after a reset, close and reopen Outlook and enter your new password when prompted.

## Common Issues

**"Your account is locked" message:** Accounts lock after 5 failed login attempts. Use the SSPR portal or contact the helpdesk to unlock. Do not keep trying — each failed attempt resets the 30-minute lockout timer.

**SSPR verification code not arriving:** Check spam folder. SMS codes are sent from +1-555-888-0000. Allow up to 2 minutes for delivery.

**Password reset successful but still can't log in:** Wait 60 seconds and try again. If the issue persists, restart your workstation to force a domain sync.
